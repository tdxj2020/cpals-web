Founded in 2015, The Congress of PALS was founded to facilitate the development of friendship and promote mutual cooperation and understanding between its Members.
Today, it seeks to further its mission to encourage Members to strive for excellence in whatever field they shall persue.

A Stake for All, Opportunities for Everyone

Members have had the opportunity to serve in leading positions, being beacons of inspiration and encouragement for fellow Members. A record of 30 different administrations have endeavoured on the missions and visions of the Congress.
It is our belief that each and everyone has a part to play in collective leadership, where inclusivity is a core principle of our association. There is unity in diversity and we shall always strive to ensure social justice and equality within our organisation.
